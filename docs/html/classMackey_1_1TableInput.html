◆ antimap

template<typename rank_t , typename diff_t >
std::vector<std::vector<int> > Mackey::TableInput< rank_t, diff_t >::antimap
protected

antimap[i] reveals the generator of NonZeroHomology[i]

antimap[i] consists of the degree, the position of the generator (0 unless noncyclic) and a multiple (if we don't have a generator, but rather the multiple of one) Example: {5,4,-2,0,2} means that we have 2 times the 0-th generator in degree k=5, n=4, m=-2.
